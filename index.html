About the map
The caregiving journey is rarely a straight line. This map gathers five themes that caregivers return to — sometimes all at once — as they walk alongside loved ones living with multiple mental health conditions.
How to use it
Tap a petal above, or a dot on the spiral below, to open a theme. Each opens with a short description, a caregiver's reflection on video, and resources you can take with you.
The upward spiral

Non-linear, but always moving forward.

You could be at any point in the spiral at once — returning to getting unstuck while you're already well into the emotional journey. The shape is deliberate: a caregiving journey loops, but it also rises.

Each dot on the spiral is a theme. Click one to open it.
